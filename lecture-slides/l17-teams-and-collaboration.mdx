## Cross-Disciplinary Teamwork

Autonomous Vehicles require:

* **Software Engineering & AI:** Algorithms, behavior planning, decision-making, machine learning, computer vision

* **Robotics & Control Engineering:** Navigation, motion planning, vehicle control systems

* **Sensor Fusion & Perception:** Integrating data from cameras, lidar, radar, GPS to interpret environment

* **Mechanical & Automotive Engineering:** Vehicle design, drive-by-wire systems, hardware integration

* **Systems Engineering:** Coordinating all components, ensuring safety, testing, infrastructure connectivity
